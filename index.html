Critical Alerts & Expectations重要選課須知與預期管理

  • 1. English Proficiency B1+ Required1. 英語能力要求 B1+ This is an EMI course. Weekly oral presentations and group discussions in English are mandatory. You must speak up every week. 這是一門全英授課 (EMI) 課程。每週皆需進行英語口頭報告與小組討論。您必須每週開口發言。
  • 2. High Workload & Attendance2. 高強度投入與出席要求 Groups will be formed in Week 1. If you must be absent on 2/25, please submit a leave request on Moodle in advance. 第一週將進行分組。若您於 2/25 必須缺席，請務必提前於 Moodle 請假區提出申請。
  • 3. Bring Your Device3. 務必攜帶筆電或平板 Please bring a laptop or tablet every week for GenAI practice. 為了進行生成式 AI 實作，請務必每週攜帶筆記型電腦或平板電腦至課堂。
  • Drop Policy退選建議機制 If you feel unprepared or overwhelmed, please drop the course early to allow waitlisted students to join. 若您覺得無法負荷或準備不及，請務必儘早退選，將名額讓給候補的同學。
